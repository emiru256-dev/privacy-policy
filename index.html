Privacy Policy for VacayBudget

Last updated: December 27, 2025

1. Introduction

VacayBudget respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile application for iOS.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Receipt Photos and Data

When you use our app to scan receipts:

  • Photos of receipts are stored locally on your device
  • Receipt information (amounts, dates, merchants, categories) is stored locally on your device
  • We do not upload or transmit your receipt photos or data to our servers

2.2 Device Information

We collect anonymous device identifiers through our payment processor (RevenueCat) to:

  • Process in-app purchases
  • Restore purchases on the same device
  • This information is not linked to your personal identity

2.3 Purchase Information

When you make in-app purchases, transaction information is processed by:

  • Apple App Store (see Apple's Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat (see RevenueCat's Privacy Policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely for:

  • Providing receipt scanning and expense tracking functionality
  • Processing and managing in-app purchases
  • Improving app performance and fixing bugs

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All receipt photos and expense data are stored locally on your device
  • You can export your data using the backup feature
  • Deleting the app will delete all locally stored data
  • We do not store your data on external servers

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For in-app purchase management. See their privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
  • Apple App Store: For payment processing. See Apple's privacy policy at https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/

6. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Share your receipt photos or expense data with third parties
  • Sell your personal information
  • Use your data for advertising or tracking purposes

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (all data is stored locally on your device)
  • Export your data using the backup feature
  • Delete your data by deleting the app

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date.
